What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences, track your activity, and improve your browsing experience. Cookies are harmless and do not contain viruses or malware.

Data Retention & Privacy

Cookies and the data they collect are subject to our Privacy Policy. By default, Google Analytics retains user-level data for 14 months. You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences.

Managing Cookies

You have full control over cookies. Most web browsers allow you to:

  • View cookies stored on your device
  • Block new cookies or opt out of specific types
  • Delete cookies after your browsing session ends
  • Clear all cookies from your browser

To manage cookies, consult the help or preferences section of your browser. Note that disabling cookies may affect website functionality.

Opting Out of Google Analytics

If you prefer not to be tracked by Google Analytics, you can install the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on. This prevents your data from being sent to Google Analytics while visiting our site.
