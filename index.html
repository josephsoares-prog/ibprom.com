Our Approach

We do not take on volume. Our practice is deliberately small — a curated portfolio of clients who share a common trait: they are building something that matters, and they need a strategic partner who has operated at the highest levels.

IBPROM was founded to bring institutional-grade advisory to the private market. Our Managing Partner has served in advisory roles to the Prime Minister of Canada, led strategic mandates for the Senate, Treasury Board, and Transport Canada, and is a Fellow of Harvard Medical School's McLean Coaching Institute.

Today, that depth of experience is deployed for a select group of founders, executives, and organizations where the work is confidential, the impact is measurable, and the relationship is long-term.
